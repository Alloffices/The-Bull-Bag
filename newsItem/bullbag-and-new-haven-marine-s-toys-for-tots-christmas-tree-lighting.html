BullBag and New Haven Marine's Toys For Tots Christmas Tree Lighting

Thursday, December 1, 2016


The BullBag team is proud to be associated with the New Haven Marine's Toys For Tots organization and will be hosting a toy drive this season on Dec 2nd at the Guilford Christmas Tree Lighting in Guilford, CT.  We will be there with our new trucks for all the kids to see and will be handing out fresh hot coffee and hot chocolate to help take the chill off of the cold evening.  Please stop by and see us and bring an unwrapped toy for a great cause.

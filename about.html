The BullBag is the ONLY  reusable and foldable dumpster bag that was designed and built to be Contractor Tough ™ . It is easily stored, simple to use and is backed by our manufacturers Lifetime Warranty. The BullBag can be filled to 3 different heights enabling you to set your budget for your project by knowing the total cost up front for disposal. The BullBag is reusable, easy to set up, will never damage your property and as long as it's used according to design, will be replaced if damaged. If you are a contractor, just give us a call! BullBag offers tiered pricing for discounts!!!

Available Sizes for Reusable Dumpster Bags & Dumpster Rentals

The BullBag is available in an 8 Cubic Yard size that can be filled to the 4, 6 or 8 Cubic Yard fill lines as marked on the BullBag - you only pay for the height filled, NOT by weight!  (BullBags hold up to 4,500 lbs!)
